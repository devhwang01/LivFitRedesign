About LivFit

LivFit was designed and developed by a dedicated team of students who recognized a growing problem: fitness-focused college students and young adults are overwhelmed by the need to juggle multiple apps just to stay healthy. We set out to fix that — and backed our design with real user research and testing.

Built with Real Users in Mind

Our research revealed that even motivated individuals find it frustrating and time-consuming to switch between different platforms for tracking workouts, meals, and progress. These barriers make it harder to stay consistent and motivated — especially for those balancing busy schedules.

LivFit solves this with a unified fitness experience that integrates personalized workout plans, nutrition logging, and progress tracking — all in one place. With feedback from actual users, we refined our features to include motivational tools like workout suggestions for missed days and popular exercises, helping users stay on track and reach their goals.
